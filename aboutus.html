About Us

Miles To Trek is a Travel and Adventure company specialized in Treks, Tours, Adventure activities and Weekend gateways etc.

What we offer is specific – unforgettable vacations/ Weekend gateways – but our focus is broad in terms of destinations and trip styles. We have a tour that fits nearly anyone who loves to trek/travel in world-renowned, breathtaking landscapes.

Why Miles To Trek

WE PROVIDE THE BEST HIKING AND TREKKING TO YOU JOURNEY

Exceptional Leaders

Our commitment is to work with good people and to prove ourselves as the best leaders in the industry. These extraordinary men and women (leaders) have the ability to turn a great getaway /trek /tour into an unforgettable, inspiring and sometimes truly life changing - adventure.

Comprehensive Risk Management

All of our guides are either certified Wilderness First Responders or first aid certified, and each carries a field-issued first aid kit, comprehensive list of medical protocols.

Transfer and Refund Policy

We understand life can be unpredictable at times, and that plans can change.

Strong Conservation Mission

Every time you travel/trek/trip with us you are helping to conserve the kids and future generations. We donate for the kids who has a dream to live a happy life.

Customer Service

We believe in time management and customer service- life is for one who bounces back on time. we're open every day, so you can get answers to your questions any day of the week.

Eco-Friendly

The dream was to serve the mountains in best possible ways (keeping mountain clean is necessity and we took it as our duty to keep it clean) later decided to start a business doing what we loved.
